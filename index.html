About

The Innovation Challenge is a semester-long program hosted by CEAS Tribunal and the Lindner Center for Entrepreneurship. Students are given the opportunity to develop an entrepreneurial idea that they have and work on it with a team of students or by themselves. This involves developing a pitch deck, creating a prototype, performing market research, and presenting final product and a 5-minute pitch to a panel of judges.

Students are eligible to receive $750 for participating in the challenge. Besides ideating and innovating, the challenge is a huge social opportunity to meet and collaborate with new people, network with industry professionals, collaborate on ideas, and become part of a community of students looking to do cool things and help create the future.

The Innovation Challenge is hosted on Wednesdays from 5:30 pm to 6:30 pm in the Kautz Attic (Lindner 4350).

How To Join

Simply show up on Wednesday at 5:30 pm in the Kautz Attic (Lindner 4350) and join us!

Feel free to fill out our interest form which asks basic information about yourself so we can get to know you and contact you in the future via your school email. Additionally, join the CEAS Tribunal Slack, which we often communicate on using the Innovation Challenge channel.

Email us at g-InnovationChallenge@mailuc.onmicrosoft.com if you have any further questions about joining or the challenge in general.
